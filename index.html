About Me

I’m a video editor specializing in Direct Response Ads, with 3+ years of experience crafting performance-driven ad creatives, alongside educational videos, organic content, and motion-based visuals.

I focus on blending fast paced editing, clean visuals, and strong storytelling to create content that's engaging, scroll stopping, and brand driven. From sharp transitions and dynamic motion graphics to sound design and pacing, I make sure every frame serves a purpose. Whether it's content for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, or ad campaigns, I craft videos that not only look great but also perform.
